**Om**nipair **F**utarchy **G**overnance token (**OMFG)** is Omnipair's ownership token. Holders have pro-rata rights to the protocol's IP, with governance over these rights enforced algorithmically through futarchy.

The protocol leverages futarchy via [MetaDAO](https://www.metadao.fi/) — a market-based governance mechanism open to any participant. Rather than token-weighted voting, decisions are made through prediction markets where participants trade on expected outcomes.

Futarchy holds control over key critical authorities:

- Treasury allocations
- OMFG mint authority
- MetaDAO tooling upgrades

Omnipair's smart contracts are immutable — they cannot be upgraded or modified after deployment. Futarchy acts as a regulatory layer, ensuring protocol governance remains grounded in market-driven economic incentives.

Learn more about how futarchy governance works in [Futarchy Authority](/api-reference/accounts/futarchy-authority).
