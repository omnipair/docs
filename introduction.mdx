## Overview

Omnipair is a decentralized [hyperstructure](https://nicogs.medium.com/hyperstructures-5e6f36c2c82b) protocol for spot & margin trading designed for permissionless, oracle-less, and isolated-collateral markets.

Built on Solana and powered by a novel [Generalized Automated Market Maker (GAMM)](/technical-breakdown/generalized-automated-market-maker) model, Omnipair introduces a mechanism where liquidity is not only used for swaps but also natively lent out to borrowers, maximizing capital efficiency in every pool.

## Why?

Traditional DeFi money markets are:

- **Permissioned**: Asset listings must be manually approved.
- **Oracle-reliant**: Introduces attack vectors and external dependencies.
- **Inefficient**:
  - AMMs leave liquidity idle;
  - Lending markets are isolated and cannot operate without external makers.

These constraints especially affect long-tail assets that lack deep integrations but command real market demand.

## How It Works

- **GAMM**: Unified pool for swaps and lending
- **EMA pricing**: Oracleless, smoothed lending price
- **Dynamic CF**: Impact-aware collateral factors
- **Internal liquidations**: Debt write-off mechanism
- **Recursive leverage**: Native leverage loops

## Vision

We believe the next era of onchain finance must be permissionless across the entire stack, driven by isolated market dynamics where any asset with liquidity can become its own collateral market by default.

This is the Uniswap moment for lending: turning fragmented, permissioned credit rails into unified markets that can be created in seconds without relying on any external oracles or governance decisions.
